# AI Startup Co-Founder Agent

## Problem Statement

Many aspiring entrepreneurs struggle to validate startup ideas before investing significant time and resources. Market research, competitor analysis, financial planning, and risk assessment often require expertise and considerable effort.

## Solution

AI Startup Co-Founder Agent is a multi-agent AI platform that evaluates startup ideas and generates investor-style reports automatically.

The system uses specialized AI agents that collaborate to provide:

* Market Research
* Competitor Analysis
* Business Strategy
* Risk Assessment
* SWOT Analysis
* Financial Projections
* MVP Planning
* Startup Scoring
* Executive Summary

## Key Features

### Market Research Agent

Analyzes market size, customer segments, trends, opportunities, and challenges.

### Competitor Analysis Agent

Identifies competitors and evaluates competitive positioning.

### Business Strategy Agent

Generates revenue models, pricing strategies, partnerships, and growth opportunities.

### Risk Assessment Agent

Evaluates technical, operational, financial, and legal risks.

### SWOT Analysis Agent

Provides strategic strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats.

### Financial Projection Agent

Generates startup cost estimates, revenue forecasts, and funding requirements.

### MVP Planning Agent

Creates a roadmap for product development and launch.

### Startup Scoring Agent

Generates startup scores and investment recommendations.

### Executive Summary Agent

Produces a concise investor-ready overview.
